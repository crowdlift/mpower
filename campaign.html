mPower Solar Generator Applications

Our M24 Solar Generator can power enough devices simultaneously for up to 1200 watts (M12: 600 watts)- that could be up to 24 full charges for a laptop! You get DC from the battery via two 5V USB ports, 12V from the two cigarette lighter connectors, and thanks to its internal inverter, you get alternating current (AC) via the two 110V (or 220V) sockets.
The mPower unique smart App will estimate the time to charge or discharge of the battery, help you orientate the solar panels with respect to the sun for optimum power generation and might even suggest you to save energy based on your typical consumption and the local weather forecast.

So what can you do with the mPower Solar Generator? Here are some applications:

Take it Camping

Use your mPower Solar Generator to power a portable cooler, an electric burner, a coffee maker, an air mattress blower or even a heated blanket to keep you warm at night. And of course you can use it to recharge all your devices from smartphones, to digital cameras and tablets.

See the table below for the battery capacity of our three models (M6, M12 & M24) for camping applications.

Use it during Blackouts

Use your mPower for critical needs such as light, coffee maker and microwave, as well as to stay connected to the world through your smartphones, tablets, laptops and Wi-Fi; and why not for entertainment powering your TV, DVR and even a game console.

See the table below for the battery capacity of our three models (M6, M12& M24) for blackout situations.

And this is not all, just about anywhere you need portable power you can consider using an mPower Solar Generator.

The solar panel charges the battery during the day and the battery provides you electricity day and night. The following day, the panel recharges the battery, restarting the process.

Key Features


Sturdy off road wheels

Sturdy, off-road wheels that don’t break and don’t sink into the sand.

Light durable casing

The mPower Solar Generator's casing is made from the lightest, yet most durable, polypropylene material on Earth. It is the same material used by the world’s top suitcase manufacturers, to ensure that almost everyone in your family can load the mPower in your trunk.

Outlets

Each mPower comes with two USB sockets and two 12V socket, and our M12 and M24 also have 110V (220V) outlets. So you can connect just about any appliance, such as a stove top, 12V thermo-electric cooler, your smartphone and more.

Power output

Our M24 mPower Solar Generator can power enough devices simultaneously for up to 1200 watts. See the application examples above for more details.

Daisy chain for more power

If you need even more power from your generator, you can daisy chain two or more mPower Solar Generators to double, triple or more, the available energy.

mPower App

The mPower is the world’s first solar generator to come with a smart App, which is very much needed to keep you powered, extend the life of the battery and deliver the best value for your investment.

The App delivers the following information:

  • Estimated time to charge or discharge of the battery.
  • How to orientate the solar panels with respect to the sun for optimum generation of power.
  • Local weather forecast for the coming days. If cloudy or rainy, the App calculates if the reduced solar generation will be enough to power your typical consumption. If needed, it alerts you so you can choose to save energy today to have more power during the upcoming cloudy days.

Our BUY 1 — DONATE 1 Model

Why “Buy 1—Donate 1”?
Because in developing countries, one billion people still depend on kerosene for light. And the burning of kerosene lamps leads to:

  • The equivalent of smoking 4 packs of cigarettes a day
  • Respiratory illnesses such as asthma, bronchitis, pneumonia and lung cancer
  • The premature death of 1.5 million people every year
  • Trapping a family in poverty—about 20% of their income goes into buying kerosene
  • Global warming—kerosene burnt in Africa releases more CO2 than all CO2 released in the UK

Within our Perks (see right column) you'll find that when you buy one M12 or one M24 Solar Generator, we'll donate in your name one M6 Solar Generator plus an LED light to one family without electricity. Enough for this family to charge their cellphones, have harmless light at night and stop using their kerosene lamp. The M6 and the LED light save them time and miles of walking, and give them a better quality of life so they can read and study to escape poverty.
This may feel like a drop in the ocean, helping a handful of people out of the billion without electricity, but your contribution will be LIFE-CHANGING for the one family that receives an mPower Solar Generator because of your purchase.

IMPORTANT NOTE: You do not pay more because of the donations.

Why? Because instead of spending money on advertising, we redirect this money and part of our profits, towards the donations.



We believe every adult and child in the world should have access to electricity and through our BUY 1 — DONATE 1 model, we aim to provide millions of units to developing countries.

Access to electricity is essential to a family’s health, safety, education, economic progress and quality of life.

Imagine what your life would be without electricity. Say goodbye to your cellphone, laptop, internet, TV, radio, music, refrigerator, air conditioning, lights at night, most medical exams, treatments, surgeries and much more.

Well, one billion people don’t need to imagine life without electricity, they experience it every day of their lives.

Product Information

The mPower comes in three sizes starting from the M6, M12, and the M24.



A quick look inside

Looking at the interior of the mPower Solar Generator you get all of the required electronics for a perfectly balanced system.

The charge controller and the battery management system optimize the transfer of electricity from the solar panels to the battery and keep the batteries properly fed and safe for the long term. The inverter provides you with the alternating current.

Manufacturing and Assembly

We source the very best components around the world to ensure that you get the very best Solar Generator your money can buy at an incredibly competitive price. For example, the world’s best solar cells of today are made in California, the best material for the case are made in Germany, and so on. Finally, we invented the mPower Solar Generator in Florida, USA, the Sunshine State, where we will continue to design, develop and ultimately assemble the product.

How do I share this campaign to get Referrals for the #LightUptheWorld Challenge?

Who can participate in the #LightUptheWorld Challenge?
Anyone from around the world that is 18 years old or older, by Apr-11-2016. General requirements are: passion for volunteerism and for helping families in need, enthusiasm about the outdoors and overcoming challenges, ability to engage with others and successfully lead teams, willingness to visit places with precarious accommodations and good written and verbal English skills

No. The only thing that matters for the #LightUptheWorld Challenge is how many 'Referrals' actually visit the campaign. They do NOT need to contribute to add 'Referrals' points to your performance in the challenge.

Of course. You can check as often as you want to. Please refer to the following instructions to do so: https://support.indiegogo.com/hc/en-us/articles/203660683

How do I know if I am within the 12 finalists in the #LightUptheWorld Challenge?
Starting May-10 we will send a weekly email informing their performance to the participants ranked within the Top 12 in the challenge (and also to those that are close to reach the Top 12). To make sure you will be receiving these emails please send us an email to community@mpowersolargenerator.com with the subject '#LightUptheWord Challenge' and your Indiegogo user name.

Are there other compensation components beyond the US$ 150,000 annual salary?

Yes! Additionally to the salary, the company will provide the mPower Ambassador with 12 round trip airfares from Applicant’s home nearest capital city, all travel expenses paid during the year of the assignment while delivering the donated solar generators and travel insurance for the same period.

Does the Buy 1-Donate 1 business model impact the retail prices of the mPower Solar Generators?
No. For-profit companies spend lots of money in advertising.  We use the advertising money (plus, a portion of our profits) for donations instead of doing advertising. We are confident that lots of caring people also want to help create a better world and one way of helping is by spreading the word about causes and products they care about.  In this way, the portion of the sale that usually gets reinvested in advertising, we can instead use it for donations resulting in a win/win/win for our customers, the company and the recipient of the donation.

Where will you send the donated mPower Solar Generators?
The units donated as a result of the current Indiegogo campaign will be delivered by the mPower Ambassador jointly with TECHO's team to families without access to electricity in Haiti. After this campaign we will evaluate and design a distribution program to bring much needed mPower Solar Generators to other countries.

What is TECHO?
TECHO.org (which means roof, in Spanish) is a youth led organization present in Latin America & the Caribbean that seeks to overcome poverty in slums through engaging the joint work of volunteers and families living in extreme poverty. Over the last 19 years, through the work and donations of more than 750,000 caring volunteers and donors, TECHO has built over 100,000 transitional homes in 19 Latin-American countries for the poorest of the poor. Upon completion, TECHO implements social inclusion programs to show these people how to escape poverty for good.

Are mPower Solar Generators available in stores?
Currently, mPower products are available exclusively on Indiegogo.

Where is the mPower Solar Generator manufactured?
We invented the mPower Solar Generator in Florida, USA, the Sunshine State, where we will continue to design, develop and ultimately assemble the product with the very best components sourced from around the world.

What are the characteristics of the main components of the mPower Solar Generator?
The mPower M12 and M24 include: crystalline silicon flexible solar panels, lithium iron phosphate (LFP) batteries with an embedded battery management system (BMS) that includes overcharge and over discharge protection, pure sine wave inverters, and thermal circuit breakers.

Do I need any additional cables or components in order to use the mPower Solar Generator?
No. The mPower Solar Generator has been designed to be ready to use by any adult without the need of additional cables or components.

Can anyone use the mPower Solar Generator?
The mPower Solar Generator generates and store electric power similar to that of the electric grid at home. Minors should never use the mPower Solar Generator without adult supervision.

Can I connect additional solar panels to the mPower Solar Generator?
Yes, the mPower M12 and M24 accept additional solar panels to be connected to them. You will need special cables available from mPower that are not included with the product since they are not part of its core functionality.

Can I get more power by connecting two mPower Solar Generators together?
Yes, you can connect (daisy-chain) two mPower Solar Generators with special cables available from mPower.

What warranty do you offer on the mPower Solar Generator?
All mPower Solar Generators have a 1 year warranty including all of the components, even the battery. The above warranty covers only defects arising under normal use and does not include malfunctions or failures resulting from misuse, abuse, neglect, alteration, problems with electrical power, usage not in accordance with product instructions, acts of nature, or improper installation or repairs made by anyone other than mPower or mPower’s authorized third-party service provider.

Where do you ship?
We ship worldwide! We make sure we ship the mPower Solar Generator according to safety transportation requirements.

Do you have a prototype ready?
Yes! We’ve built a fully-functional prototype following precise UL Certification guidelines and then produced a 3D printed case to fine-tune the design and test the connecting parts.
